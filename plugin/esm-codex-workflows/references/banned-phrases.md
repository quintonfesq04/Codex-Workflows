# Banned Phrases

Avoid these in ESM-style output:

- em dashes
- storyline
- narrative
- ultimately
- at the end of the day
- it's worth noting
- furthermore
- in conclusion
- showcased
- displayed
- demonstrated
- solidified
- cemented
- highlighted
- trajectory
- this is a player who
- this is a team that
- absolute dominance when the stats are not dominant
