# Player Report Carousel Format

Use this preset for breakout reports, slump checks, injury returns, prospect updates, role changes, and player status posts that are not mainly a rumor or list.

## Trigger Examples

- `Format: player report`
- `Make a player report carousel for Bryce Eldridge`
- `Build a breakout report carousel`
- `Make an injury update carousel for Acuna`

## Best For

- Single-player updates.
- Prospect arrival or franchise-face posts.
- Injury concern or return posts.
- "Is this real?" player performance checks.
- Clear story plus supporting stats.

## Default Slide Map

1. Cover: player/topic hook.
2. What happened: newest event or reason for the post.
3. Production: current line or strongest stat proof.
4. Context: age, role, team situation, schedule, or underlying reason.
5. Debate: why it may be real, risky, or changing fast.
6. Final takeaway or meme.

## Aesthetic Direction

- Flexible middle ground between analytical and meme-heavy.
- Cover can use equal-sized lines or the smaller-top-line/bigger-bottom-word look depending on the phrase.
- Use team-color gradients when the player identity is the main selling point.
- Use black gradients when the headline is more dramatic or debate-driven.
- Info slides should keep the player image dominant and copy concise.

## Required Inputs

- Team and exact hex colors.
- Cover text.
- Cover image and slide images.
- Copy and bottom stat lines.
- Meme still if using a meme finish.

## Verification

- The first info slide explains why the post exists right now.
- The stat slide supports the cover promise.
- Final slide leaves the viewer with a clear take or debate.
- No slide repeats an image accidentally when enough images were supplied.
