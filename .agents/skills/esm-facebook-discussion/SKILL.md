# ESM Facebook Discussion

## Trigger Examples

- `Make a Facebook discussion post about the Mets bullpen`
- `Give me a Facebook post about Soto's slump`
- `Make this into a debate post for Facebook`

## Workflow

1. Verify the current sports context.
2. Choose a topic that invites real comments without engagement bait.
3. Keep the post short and readable.
4. Lead with the tension or question.
5. Add one or two supporting facts.
6. Close with a clear discussion question.
7. Avoid misleading framing and unoriginal reposting.

## Output Rules

- Text-first unless user asks for media.
- No long source list in the post.
- No bait such as `COMMENT YES OR NO`.
- No copied tweets.
- Use original analysis and framing.

## Verification

- Confirm facts are current.
- Confirm the post is not duplicative or misleading.
- Confirm it has one clear discussion question.
