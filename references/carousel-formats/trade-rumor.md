# Trade And Rumor Carousel Format

Use this preset for deadline names, trade reports, extension friction, clubhouse reports, contract pressure, and "could this player move?" posts.

## Trigger Examples

- `Format: trade`
- `Make a trade rumor carousel`
- `Deadline lottery carousel for Sandy Alcantara`
- `Could Ketel Marte be traded?`

## Best For

- Report-driven posts.
- Deadline market speculation.
- Clubhouse or rest-day frustration reports.
- Buy/sell team debates.
- Player value versus relationship/contract tension.

## Default Slide Map

1. Cover: question or market hook.
2. Report/context: what was said or what changed.
3. Player value: why the player still matters.
4. Complication: why the situation is messy.
5. Meme: fan or front-office reaction.
6. Verdict/market: what the team has to decide.

## Aesthetic Direction

- High-impact cover with serious deadline energy.
- Strong bottom gradient on cover. Trade hooks usually benefit from black gradient plus team-color second line, or a vivid primary team-color gradient.
- Info slides should balance clean reporting with visual drama.
- Bottom stat should summarize the market case: record, contract option, OPS, ERA, standings, or report label.
- Meme slide should break the tension without replacing the actual report.

## Required Inputs

- Team and exact hex colors.
- Cover text.
- Source/report copy if the user wants a report summarized.
- Player/team images in intended order.
- Meme still and caption.
- Final stats if user does not want research.

## Verification

- Report wording is attributed or framed carefully when needed.
- If the user provides final report copy, do not verify or rewrite the claim beyond layout fit.
- The trade question remains clear across the carousel.
- Meme slide is included unless the user explicitly skips it.
